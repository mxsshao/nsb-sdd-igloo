New Calculation

  1. On the Main Screen, Click on the 'New' button.

  2. The 'New Calculation Screen' will open.

  3. Enter the internal radius of the Igloo Dome into the text box.

    Note: Values must be between 0.50 and 1000 metres

  4. Enter the length of the Entrance Tunnel into the text box.

    Note: Values must be between 0.10 and 100 metres

  5. Then click Calculate to start the calculation.

  6. Wait while the program calculates the number of bricks.

  7. The 'Results Screen' will open after the calculation has been completed
